Decomposition Recycling

How to choose between Recycling and Decomposing?

Recycling is basically to break down and reuse component materials.

Items that can be recycled are:

  1. Paper: office paper, magazines, newspapers and junk mail
  2. Green, clear and brown glass bottles and jars
  3. Juice and milk cartons
  4. Electronic Items (E-Waste)
  5. Garden Waste and Organics
  6. Steel (tin) and aluminium cans and empty aerosols

While decomposition is to separate or break down
something into its components and to disintegrate or fragment.

Items that need to be decomposed are:

  1. Plastic bags
  2. Takeaway coffee cups
  3. Polystyrene (foam)
  4. Bubble wrap
  5. Syringes or medical waste
  6. Ceramics, ovenware or light bulbs
